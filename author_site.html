About Me

S. M. Hollierre is a bilingual BiPoC author based in Germany. Years of work in the therapeutic field with emotionally challenged youth have shaped her fascination with human defense mechanisms and the psychology behind power dynamics.

This experience informs her stories, where she explores the complex dynamics of found families—how people choose to anchor each other when traditional structures fail. She delves into the lives of characters in high-stakes situations, focusing on the universal search for a place where one can exist without a mask.

The Richter Scale

Genre: Upmarket Financial Thriller / Contemporary Romance Word Count: ~80,000 Status: Complete & Polished

To save his mentor's empire, a brilliant financial analyst must get close to the unpredictable heir of a rival dynasty. But in a world of secrets, the greatest risk isn't losing money—it's trusting the one person he's supposed to destroy.

Blurb

When an anonymous leak threatens the empire of his mentor, Jon Carver, brilliant financial analyst Lucas Castro doesn't hesitate. He answers the call of his found family, flying to Zurich only to find himself at the center of a conspiracy that goes far beyond tax evasion.

His investigation leads him straight to Bençe Richter, the enigmatic heir of a rival clan and the key to the entire affair. What starts as a strategic analysis quickly becomes a dangerous psychological duel. In the glittering, ice-cold world of St. Moritz, Lucas must decide who he can trust while an unexpected attraction threatens to break all the rules. "The Richter Scale" is a gripping thriller about the power of loyalty, the price of truth, and the discovery that the most dangerous tremors aren't financial.

A Connected Universe

Every couple featured as main cast in "The Richter Scale" has their own story to tell. Jon and Ari's journey unfolds over three manuscripts, Lucas and Bençe's story is the one you are reading now, and Maxwell and André's story is waiting to be told.

This is a universe I am deeply committed to exploring, with a rich backlog of completed and planned works that I am excited to develop further.
